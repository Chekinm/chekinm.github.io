Abraham Lempel and his compression algorithm

On 4.02.2023, I heard a sad news that Abraham Lempel has died. He was 86. The news say that he is a grand father of all modern compresion algorithms. Today, everybody knows evrybody knows words mp3, jpeg, or zip. But just a few know who invented this thing for us.

I decided to dedicate my project to Agraham. In our journey we will meet prof Abraham Lempel, learn the original idea of the compresion algorithm. We even write a simple implementation of his algoritm in python.
